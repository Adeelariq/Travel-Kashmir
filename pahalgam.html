Pahalgam - A Tranquil Escape

Introduction to Pahalgam

Pahalgam, often called the "Valley of Shepherds," is a serene town located in the Anantnag district of Jammu and Kashmir. It is a popular tourist destination known for its breathtaking landscapes, lush green meadows, and crystal-clear rivers. The town serves as a gateway to the famous Amarnath Yatra and is also renowned for trekking, fishing, and horse riding.

Attractions in Pahalgam

Pahalgam offers a variety of attractions for tourists. Some of the notable ones include:

  • Baisaran Valley: Known as "Mini Switzerland" for its lush meadows and panoramic views.
  • Betaab Valley: A picturesque valley with beautiful views of mountains and valleys.
  • Sheshnag Lake: A stunning alpine lake that attracts trekkers and nature lovers.

Activities in Pahalgam

Visitors can enjoy a wide range of activities in Pahalgam, including:

  • Trekking to the nearby meadows and mountains.
  • Fishing in the Lidder River, famous for its trout.
  • Horse riding through lush green landscapes.
